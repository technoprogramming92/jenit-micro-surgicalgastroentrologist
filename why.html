Experties of Dr. Jenit Gandhi in Bariatric Surgery

Bariatric surgery is a collection of weight-loss procedures designed to alter the digestive system, helping individuals with severe obesity achieve significant and sustained weight loss.

These surgeries include options like Intra Gastric Balloon Insertion, which reduces stomach capacity without surgery, and Laparoscopic Adjustable Gastric Banding, which places a band around the stomach to limit food intake.

More advanced procedures, such as Laparoscopic Sleeve Gastrectomy and Laparoscopic Roux-En Y Gastric Bypass, involve altering the stomach and intestines to promote weight loss and improve metabolic health.

Dr. Jenit Gandhi, a leading expert in these procedures, also performs specialized surgeries like Single Incision Sleeve Gastrectomy, Laparoscopic Duodenal Switch, and Metabolic Surgery for Diabetes, ensuring tailored, effective treatments for each patient.

Whether you're considering a first-time procedure or need a Revisional/Redo Bariatric Surgery, Dr. Gandhi’s expertise as one of the best surgeons in this field guarantees optimal outcomes and compassionate care.

Experties of Dr. Jenit Gandhi in HPB Surgeries

HPB (Hepato-Pancreato-Biliary) surgeries involve complex procedures focused on the liver, pancreas, bile ducts, and gallbladder.

These surgeries are critical in treating cancers and other serious conditions affecting these organs. Dr. Jenit Gandhi is a leading expert in HPB surgeries, offering advanced treatment options for conditions such as Cholangiocarcinoma (Bile Duct Cancer), Biliary Stricture, and Liver Cancer.

His proficiency extends to the surgical management of Choledochal Cysts, where he applies precise and minimally invasive techniques to ensure the best outcomes for his patients.

As one of the most skilled surgeons in this specialized field, Dr. Gandhi provides compassionate care, utilizing advanced technology to perform these life-saving procedures.
